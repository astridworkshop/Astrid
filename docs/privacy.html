Privacy Policy

Your data stays where it belongs — with you.

The short version: Astrid collects absolutely nothing. No analytics, no accounts, no telemetry. Every conversation stays on your own hardware and network. This policy exists to make that promise explicit.

No Data Collection

Astrid AI Client does not collect, transmit, store, or share any personal data. All conversations occur exclusively between your iOS device and the local AI server you configure on your own hardware and network. Nothing is routed through Astrid Workshop servers or any third-party service.

No Accounts Required

Astrid requires no user accounts, registration, email address, or sign-in of any kind. You can use the app fully without providing any identifying information.

Local Storage Only

Chat history, profiles, and app settings are stored locally on your device only. This data never leaves your device or local network. It is not backed up to any external service by Astrid.

No Third-Party Services

Astrid does not integrate any analytics platforms, advertising networks, crash reporting services, or third-party tracking of any kind. The app makes no outbound network requests except to the local server address you explicitly configure.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes in a future version of Astrid, the updated policy will be published here and the "Last updated" date below will reflect the change.
